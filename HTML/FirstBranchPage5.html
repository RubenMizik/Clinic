Overview

Welcome to the Health Declaration and Appointment System! This tool helps students, teachers, and staff report symptoms safely. Follow the steps below to get started.

How to Report Symptoms

Use the Health Declaration Form to report any current symptoms. This ensures a safe environment for everyone.

  1. Navigate to the Health Declaration Form page.
  2. Fill in your Personal Information (e.g., name, age, grade). Required fields will show up with an exclamation mark (!).
  3. In the Current Symptoms section:
    • Check the boxes for any symptoms you or the person you're reporting for is experiencing (e.g., Fever, Cough).
    • For symptoms not listed or you dont understand what you're currently experiencing or feeling, enter them and explain them in the custom input field and click "Add" (confirm when prompted).
    • Note: You can only select any of the symptoms and you cannot leave it None or Blank, you atleast need to explain what you're currently feeling for the nurses to prepare in advance to treat you fast.
  4. Click "Submit Symptom Report". You'll see a confirmation message, and the page will refresh automatically.
  5. If you have questions, contact the clinic staff.

Tip: Selected symptoms will display as tags before submission for review.

Tip: Clinic hours are typically 8 AM to 5 PM on weekdays. Weekends may be unavailable.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
